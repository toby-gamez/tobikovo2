Past simple and event

Past event

Dělení

  • There is -> there was (there wasn't, there was not)
  • There are -> there were (there weren't, there were not)

Past simple

Používáme, když chceme vysvětlit něco, co se stalo v minulém čase na specifickém místě a času.

Pravidelná slovesa

  • dance -> danced
  • call -> called
  • study -> studied
  • plan -> planned

Nepravidelná slovesa

Ty se musíme naučit. Třeba na straně 112 v učebnici English Plus 1 (second edition), Oxford.

Časové výrazy

  • 3 days, 5 minutes, one year, a month - ago
  • last - month, year, summer, week
  • yesterday, the day before yesterday (předevčírem), when I was 10, on December 23rd
